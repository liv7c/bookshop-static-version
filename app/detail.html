Wiener Werkstätte

ZÜLO W (Franz von)Die 12 Monate [The Twelve Months]. Originallithographien.

(Vienna), Druck und Verlag der Wiener W erkstätte, n. d. (1912).

4to, loose as issued in publisher’s black cloth portfolio illustrated with an original colour lithograph by the artist mounted on each side, abstract handpainted paper doublures. Suite of 13 ORIGINAL COLOUR LITHOGRAPHS BY FRANZ VON ZÜLOW, including 1 plate depicting the four seasons and 12 plates for the months. Complete. Together, 15 colourful and imaginative lithographs with multiple subjects incorporating themes of popular culture and folklore, astrological signs, animals and varied ornaments.

FIRST EDITION printed in small number. Franz von Zülow (1883-1963), Austrian painter, designer, graphic artist and muralist, member of the Secession mouvement. Studied at the Kunstgewerbeschule (School of Applied Arts) with F. von Myrbach, Carl Otto Czeschka and A. von Stark. From 1908 he was an illustrator and designer for the Wiener Werkstätte where he created fabrics, fans, painted boxes and screens.

Two plates with left margin a bit short, an extremely fresh and fine copy of A RARE AND DESIRABLE WORK PRODUCED AT THE CELEBRATED WIENER WERKSTÄTTE.

Ref. Catalogue of the Cotsen Children’s Library, 12371 / Heller, Die Bunte Welt, 455 / Schweiger, Wiener Werkstätte, p.185
